Zain Physics LMS

A scalable educational platform for physics, engineered to handle thousands of concurrent students and automated transactions.

2024 Laravel, MySQL, Blade 5k+ Students

The Challenge

The client needed to move away from manual course management to an automated system capable of handling high-traffic exam periods and secure video delivery for over 5,000 students.

The Solution

I built a custom LMS on Laravel with a high-performance backend. Key features included an automated payment gateway, a secure content delivery system, and an optimized student dashboard.
